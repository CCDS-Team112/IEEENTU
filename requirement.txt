Sprint 1 minimal: Symptom intake function (with AI support)

Goal

Collect a userâ€™s symptoms quickly and safely, then produce a structured record that can be used for rule based triage and for a doctor summary.

User flows

Flow A: Form intake (no AI)

User taps Symptom check

User fills a short form

App runs rule based triage and shows next steps

Save session

Flow B: AI assisted intake

User taps AI help

User types or speaks symptoms

AI asks a few fixed follow up questions

App shows a review screen with fields pre filled

User confirms

App runs rule based triage and shows next steps

Save session

Minimal fields to collect

Required

Age band: under 3 months, 3 to 12 months, 1 to 12 years, 13 to 64, 65 plus

Main symptom(s): choose from list plus optional free text

Duration: hours or days

Severity: mild, moderate, severe

Red flag checks (yes or no)

trouble breathing

severe allergic reaction signs

Validation rules

Must have at least one symptom

Duration must be positive and within a sane max (for example less than 365 days)

If age under 3 months and fever is present, mark as urgent

If any red flag is true, triage must be emergency now

Success criteria

Form completion under 2 minutes median

AI assisted completion under 3 minutes median

Saved record always contains the required fields
